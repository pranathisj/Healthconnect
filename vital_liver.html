Liver: Key Tests

Liver Function Test (LFT)

Measures enzymes, proteins, and bilirubin to assess liver health and detect damage.

Ultrasound Abdomen

Imaging test to visualize liver size, shape, and detect fatty liver or masses.

Hepatitis B Surface Antigen

Screens for hepatitis B infection, a common cause of liver disease.
