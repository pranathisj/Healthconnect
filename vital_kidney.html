Kidney: Key Tests

Serum Creatinine

Measures kidney function by assessing the level of creatinine in the blood.

Urine Routine

Checks for protein, blood, and other abnormalities in urine indicating kidney health.

eGFR (Estimated Glomerular Filtration Rate)

Estimates how well the kidneys are filtering waste from the blood.
