Heart: Key Tests

ECG (Electrocardiogram)

Measures electrical activity of the heart to detect arrhythmias, heart attacks, and other cardiac issues.

Lipid Profile

Assesses cholesterol and triglyceride levels to evaluate risk of heart disease.

Troponin Test

Detects heart muscle injury, especially after chest pain or suspected heart attack.
