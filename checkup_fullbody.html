Full Body Checkup

Comprehensive health assessment with complete diagnostic workup and specialist consultation.

What's Included

Blood Tests

  • Complete Blood Count (CBC)
  • Lipid Profile (Cholesterol, Triglycerides)
  • Liver Function Tests (LFT)
  • Kidney Function Tests (RFT)
  • Blood Sugar (Fasting & Random)
  • Thyroid Profile (TSH, Free T3, Free T4)

Clinical Examination

  • Blood Pressure & Pulse
  • Height & Weight (BMI calculation)
  • Physical Examination
  • Chest & Abdomen Assessment

Consultation

  • Detailed Doctor Consultation (30 mins)
  • Health Risk Assessment
  • Preventive Health Recommendations
  • Wellness Plan

Reports

  • Comprehensive Health Report
  • Digital Health Records
  • Follow-up Guidance

Ideal For

Adults of all ages seeking preventive health screening, regular annual checkups, or those with family history of chronic diseases.

Duration

2-3 hours (including tests and consultation)

Age Group

18 years and above
