Women's Health Checkup

Specialized comprehensive screening designed specifically for women's wellness and preventive health.

What's Included

Gynecology & Reproductive Health

  • Gynecological Examination
  • Pap Smear / Cervical Cancer Screening
  • Breast Examination
  • Mammography (if age 40+)
  • Ultrasound - Pelvis

Blood Tests

  • Complete Blood Count
  • Hormonal Panel (FSH, LH, Estrogen, Progesterone)
  • Thyroid Profile
  • Lipid Profile
  • Blood Sugar Levels
  • Liver & Kidney Function

Vital Signs & Assessment

  • Blood Pressure Monitoring
  • BMI & Body Composition
  • Bone Density Screening (if age 45+)

Consultation

  • Gynecologist Consultation (30 mins)
  • Nutritionist Guidance
  • Wellness Recommendations
  • Fertility & Pregnancy Planning (if needed)

Ideal For

Women of all ages seeking comprehensive reproductive health screening, pre-marital checkups, annual wellness exams, or those planning pregnancy.

Duration

2.5-3.5 hours (including tests and consultations)

Age Group

18 years and above
