Allergy Checkup

Comprehensive allergy testing and management plan to identify and manage your allergies effectively.

What's Included

Allergy Testing

  • Comprehensive Skin Prick Test (60+ allergens)
  • Intradermal Testing (if needed)
  • Blood Allergy Testing (IgE)
  • Panel: Environmental, Food, and Inhalant Allergens

Diagnostic Tests

  • Complete Blood Count
  • Eosinophil Count
  • Pulmonary Function Test (if respiratory symptoms)
  • Peak Flow Measurement

Clinical Assessment

  • Detailed Allergy History
  • Physical Examination
  • Asthma Risk Assessment
  • Medication Review

Management & Consultation

  • Allergist Consultation (45 mins)
  • Personalized Management Plan
  • Medication Recommendations
  • Lifestyle & Dietary Advice
  • Immunotherapy Guidance (if applicable)

Ideal For

Anyone experiencing allergic symptoms including hay fever, food allergies, asthma, eczema, or recurrent allergic reactions.

Duration

2-2.5 hours (including testing and consultation)

Age Group

5 years and above
