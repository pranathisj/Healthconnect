Blood: Key Tests

Complete Blood Count (CBC)

Measures red cells, white cells, and platelets to detect anemia, infection, and more.

ESR (Erythrocyte Sedimentation Rate)

Detects inflammation in the body, useful for diagnosing various conditions.

Blood Grouping & Typing

Determines blood type, important for transfusions and pregnancy planning.
